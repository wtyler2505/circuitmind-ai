# Product Guidelines

## User Persona
- **User:** Tyler
- **Background:** Self-taught, hands-on experience with electronics.
- **Communication Needs:** Plain language, concrete examples, no unnecessary jargon. Explicitly define acronyms.

## Design Philosophy
- **Aesthetic:** Cyberpunk / Sci-Fi. Green text on dark backgrounds.
- **UX Priority:** Clarity and Correctness. "Measure twice, cut once."
- **Privacy:** Zero telemetry. All AI calls must be user-initiated and transparent.

## Development Standards
- **Quality:** Correctness over speed. No deadlines.
- **Verification:** Mandatory testing (Unit & Visual) before completion.
- **Safety:** Treat AI content as untrusted. Sandboxed execution where possible.
